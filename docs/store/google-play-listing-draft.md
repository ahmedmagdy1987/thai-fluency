## Full description
> Tuk Talk Thai helps you speak the Thai you actually need for travel and daily
> life in Thailand. It is built speak-first: phonetics and English are shown with
> every card, and Thai script is there when you want it.
>
> Learn in short, focused sessions that fit around a real day. A guided path takes
> you from survival basics to more confident everyday conversation, and smart
> review keeps the right words coming back at the right time.
>
> WHAT YOU GET
> - A guided path across 8 stages, from first greetings to everyday conversation
> - 96 short mini-units so you always know what to do next
> - A tap-to-build Sentence Builder that helps word order click
> - Smart spaced review that brings cards back before you forget them
> - Stage Challenges to check what you have learned, in both directions
> - Daily quests, streaks, and achievements to keep momentum
> - Friendly celebrations and XP that make practice feel good
> - Sync your progress across devices with a free account
>
> HOW IT WORKS
> Start with the guided path, practice with smart review, and test yourself with
> Stage Challenges. Audio uses your device text-to-speech so you can hear phrases
> as you learn. Everything is designed for short daily sessions.
>
> HONEST NOTE
> This is an early beta. Lessons cover practical, real-world Thai, and we are still
> refining content with native review. Tuk Talk Thai helps you build real speaking
> confidence with steady practice. It does not promise instant or guaranteed
> fluency, and it is not a certification program. Please use the in-app Feedback
> option to report anything that needs a fix.

## Notes for the Data Safety form (owner to complete in Play Console)
Declare based on actual behavior. See `data-safety-privacy-checklist.md` for detail.
- Account email: collected, used for account creation and sign-in, linked to the
  user identity. (Supabase authentication.)
- App activity / learning progress: collected and synced for signed-in users
  (XP, streak, completed units, settings, challenge aggregates).
- App settings / preferences: collected and synced for signed-in users.
- Push notification token (OneSignal): collected only if the user enables
  notifications. Optional.
- Data is sent over HTTPS. No ads are shown. No data is sold to third parties.
- Users can request account deletion at /delete-account (currently a manual flow).
- Owner must verify: presence of any analytics or crash-reporting SDK, exact data
  retention windows, and whether data is processed by named service providers.
