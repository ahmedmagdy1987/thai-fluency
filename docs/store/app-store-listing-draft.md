## Description
> Tuk Talk Thai helps you speak the Thai you actually need for travel and daily
> life in Thailand. It is built speak-first: phonetics and English appear with every
> card, and Thai script is there when you want it.
>
> Learn in short, focused sessions. A guided path takes you from survival basics to
> more confident everyday conversation, and smart review keeps the right words
> coming back at the right time.
>
> WHAT YOU GET
> - A guided path across 8 stages, from first greetings to everyday conversation
> - 96 short mini-units so you always know the next step
> - A tap-to-build Sentence Builder that makes word order click
> - Smart spaced review that brings cards back before you forget
> - Stage Challenges to check what you have learned, in both directions
> - Daily quests, streaks, and achievements to keep momentum
> - Friendly celebrations and XP that make practice feel good
> - Progress sync across devices with a free account
>
> HOW IT WORKS
> Start with the guided path, practice with smart review, and test yourself with
> Stage Challenges. Audio uses your device text-to-speech so you can hear phrases as
> you learn. Everything is designed for short daily sessions.
>
> HONEST NOTE
> This is an early beta. Content is practical and still being refined with native
> review. Tuk Talk Thai helps you build real speaking confidence with steady
> practice. It does not promise instant or guaranteed fluency and is not a
> certification program. Use the in-app Feedback option to report anything that
> needs a fix.
